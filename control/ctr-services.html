Our Services

Doing the laundry is a step by step process.

Identify
We sort the fabric by colour and share (Light, White & Dark) to ensure that colour mix-up and fading can be avoided.
Wash
We wash the clothes with state-of-the-art technology to ensure the clothes are clean and fresh. We ensure the stains are removed and the fabric is fresh.
Dry
We dry your clothes in the hygienic environment as per the quality and material of the fabric. We care for your fabric just as you do.
Steam Iron
We only use steam iron with (at) appropriate (temperature not heat) heat which is gentle on your clothes and hard on the wrinkles.
Packing
Once all the process is done we pack all your belongings and it is ready to be delivered On-time!
Delivery
We always deliver your belongings carefully to make sure you smile whenever you receive your clothes. This is our Guarantee!
