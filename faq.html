FAQ

How It Works

How do I start?

All you need to do is visit our website and create an account or sign in through Facebook or simply call us on 7774005679 or feel free to visit as at our nearest store.

Where shall I drop-off my clothes?

Please choose while placing your order if you would like us to pick it up from your doorstep or concierge. We service Apartments, Condos, Businesses and houses. Please place your laundry in a bag and label it with your name and address or if it’s your second order, then you would have a reusable garment bag from KwickWash Laundry that you can reuse.

How will I know that my laundry has been picked-up?

Once you place your order with KwickWash Laundry, Riders gets notified that your laundry is ready for pick-up. Our team will swing by to collect it from your concierge or doorstep (as per your chosen option) and we will change the status of your order to "Picked-up" in your account.

How will I know that my laundry has been washed and delivered?

KwickWash will send you an email or SMS to let you know your clean, fresh laundry is ready for collection as per your request. That can be either your doorstep at your house, condo or apartment building or at your concierge or you can collect it at your own convenience.

What if I don't have a concierge?

A scheduling system will pop-up and you can schedule for a pick-up time from your Door-Step.

My concierge doesn't accept laundry. What shall I do?

While placing your order, please choose a Doorstep pick-up if your concierge doesn't accept you dropping it off.

What shall I do if I have a big order with many bags?

More the merrier! Just let us know by either calling us at 7774005679 or drop us an email at contactus@kwickwash.in and we will do the needful.
My Order

How do I place an order?

To place your first order, simply visit our website and create an account – you also have the option of signing in through Facebook or Google+. Once your account is set up, you can place an order whenever you like by clicking the ‘Place Order’ tab on our website.

How do I prepare my order for pick-up?

After submitting your order, place your dry cleaning or laundry in a bag with your name, address and phone number (If any). Your name and phone number is only required for your first order, KwickWash Laundry will return your clothes in a reusable Eco-friendly bag with a customer identification tag that you can use for future orders.

How can I let you know about specific requests?

If there are any special care instructions not included on the label, you can let us know when placing your order by including your comments in the ‘Special Instructions’ section.

Do I need to count the items in my order?

Absolutely not, counting items is all part of the service. KwickWash Laundry will take care of that on your behalf.

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, the minimum order is Rs. 300. The minimum order for Wash and Fold / Wash and Iron is 3Kg. If your order is less than the minimum, we will charge you the minimum order. One exception is when you have opted into both services (Wash and Iron+Dry Clean), then only the Wash and Iron minimum would apply.

Can I cancel my order?

If you decide to cancel your order, please do so within 24 hours of placing your order either by emailing info@kwickwash.com, otherwise your order will be processed. If we attempt to pick-up and weren't able to do so, we will charge you Rs.100 "Unavailable Claim". We don't accept any text messages.

How fast will you process my order?

Turnaround time is usually 48-72 hours, not including Sundays and public holidays. We also provide a rushed service for an additional charge of Rs.300. Rushed Service is currently only offered for Wash and Fold service.

Do you have monthly laundry packages?

We most certainly do. We have four packages that you can chose from that you can purchase directly on our website. We have the Bronze, Silver, Gold and Diamond packages. You can also contact us to assist you with setting up your account.

How will I know my order was received?

You will receive an email receipt or sms confirmation from KwickWash Laundry within five minutes of placing your order

What if I do not receive an email receipt?

If you don’t receive an email or sms, please check your spam folder and add info@kwickwash.com to your approved senders list). If you don’t receive an email receipt or confirmation please email us at info@kwickwash.com

What happens to my laundry bag?

Please place your order in a disposable bag, as KwickWash Laundry won’t take responsibility of your laundry bags, hampers, suit bags in case they get misplaced. We deliver your clothes in a reusable Eco-friendly garment bag, which has your personalized name tag for future use. Therefore, please don’t give out clothes in expensive suit covers, hampers or bags.

What if I am not home for scheduled delivery?

The driver will give you a call at the time of delivery and if you are not there please let us know at call where to leave the clothes.

What happens if driver attempts to pick-up or drop-off and fails?

If an attempt of a pick-up fails, then the customer is charged a Rs.100 "Unavailable Claim".

How do I know if the driver is coming to pick my order?

Once the driver starts a pickup task, the customer will receive an SMS notification informing them that the driver is on his way. The SMS message will include drivers name and phone number in case customer needs to contact driver.

What happens if I don’t sort my order between Dry Clean and Wash & Iron?

Don’t worry we will sort the clothes for you but it is the customers responsibility to let us know what items are for Dry Cleaning and what are for wash and iron. Incase you do not tell us we will chose the Dry Clean service by default.
Services

What services do you provide?

KwickWash Laundry provides the following services: Dry Clean, Wash & Fold, Wash & Iron, Shoe Laundry, Carpet Cleaning and Sofa Cleaning. We also provide a rushed service for an additional charge of Rs.300 for Wash and Fold/Iron service only.

What is the difference between Wash and Fold and Wash and Iron?

With the Wash and Fold service, we would wash, dry in the dryer and fold all your clothes. The Wash and Iron service, we would wash your clothes, dry in the dryer and Iron them.

Is your dry cleaning process Eco-friendly?

It most certainly is. We apply wet washing to all your dry cleaning items.

Can I add special instructions to my order?

You most certainly can do that. While placing your order, please add any instructions in the special instructions box and we will adhere to them. We have many customers that prefer we don't use any scented detergents in our washing because of allergies.
Wash and Fold Packages

What is a Wash and Fold/ Iron Package?

Wash and Fold package is a recurring subscription plan for your wash and fold needs i.e. Washing, drying and neatly folding/Ironing your clothes. This is a monthly subscription recurring service whereby you set the date for pickups and delivery and the frequency of these pickups within a certain month.

How can I subscribe to your package?

The plans can be subscribed by purchasing one of our plans. We offer Bronze, Silver, Gold and Diamond packages. After purchasing your selected package, please place an order online and chose the Repeat button to choose the frequency of your order. This exact order will be replicated according to the frequency chosen.

What are the available packages?

The available packages are Silver (10Kg), Gold (15Kg) and Diamond (20Kg). Each package has a different price point per Kilogram.

What are the pricing for the different package?

The pricing for the packages are as follows :-
  • Gold Package ........... Rs.190 per month.
  • Silver Package ......... Rs.110 per month.
  • Diamond Package ........ Rs.350 per month.
  • Business Package ....... Please Contact us.

Please note that the minimum order of 3Kg/Pickup applies on all orders regardless of the package that you subscribe to.

How does the charging happen?

Once the order has been delivered, we will directly deduct from the total amount from your package in advance.

What if I go above the monthly package?

If you go above the kilogram for the plan that you have chosen, then you will be charged for the extra kilogram at the rate of the plan that you have subscribed to.

How can I unsubscribe from the service?

You can unsubscribe by contacting us through phone or email at info@kwickwash.com.

How can I pause the plans if I travel?

You can pause the plans by going into your account and choosing the pause function within your orders.

What if I don’t reach utilise my package within that month?

If you don't fully utilize your credit, no credit will be allowed to be carried to the next month.

Besides cleaning, how else will KwickWash Laundry care for my clothes?

In addition to cleaning, we perform a thorough inspection of your clothes and ensure any stains, minor repairs, and wrinkles are taken care of. Rest assured, your clothes will be in tip-top condition when we return them to you.

Flexible time-slot is convenient for those customers who are available throughout the evening. This provides the drive ample time to pickup your order and wouldn't be rushed.

What is Return/Refund Policy?

KwickWash Laundry strives to provide the highest quality dry cleaning and laundering services. In the event you are not fully satisfied, please contact us by email at info@kwickwash.com and we will do our best to rectify the situation and earn back your trust.

What happens if my laundry is lost or damaged?

If your items are lost, please contact us directly and provide your order number. We will investigate what happened and ensure your satisfaction with the resolution. While we take the utmost care to provide an outstanding customer experience, occasionally things do go wrong and items can go missing or suffer damages. In these rare instances, the following guidelines dictate how customers are reimbursed and compensated for missing or damaged items:

Damaged Items

If an item is damaged through our delivery or cleaning process, we will send your damaged item to a third party clothing repair specialist for assessment and repair.
If the item can be successfully repaired, then we will repair it free of charge. If the damage cannot be repaired and was a result of the delivery/cleaning process, then we will assume full responsibility and provide 30% of the current value of garments
  • Normal wear and tear, which includes slight shrinkage, slight fading, thinning of fabric, small rips or tears from repeated laundering, and colour discoloration due to deodorant, perfumes or colognes.
If the item(s) in question were damaged due to “normal wear and tear”, then we will not provide compensation for the item(s).
